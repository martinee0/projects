About Me!

Since this is run by me I might as well introduce myself! For now until I think of something cooler, my name is Matt and I have been gaming since 2004. I won the lottery of millennial big brother who introduced me to cool classics and put me onto this path of a broad genre interest. As for what specifically I am into, the range is literally further than the eye can see (with some exclusions). I've been following most of the big AAA releases from companies such as:

  • And many more
